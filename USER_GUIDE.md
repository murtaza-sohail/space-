# CloudVault User Guide: Step-by-Step

Welcome to CloudVault! Follow these steps to explore all the premium features designed for your cloud storage simulation.

## 1. Link Your Google Account
To unlock the full potential and simulate "Cloud Sync" status:
*   Find the **"Link Google Account"** button at the bottom of the sidebar.
*   Enter a demo email (e.g., `user@gmail.com`).
*   Click **"Sign in with Google"**.
*   Your profile will appear, and you'll see the **"CLOUD SYNC"** status active!

## 2. Organize Your Storage
*   **Create Folders:** Click the **"Create Folder"** button in the sidebar. Give it a name like "Projects" and hit create.
*   **Upload Files:** Click **"Upload Files"** or simply drag and drop files directly into the file list area.
*   **Navigate:** Click on any folder to enter it. Use the breadcrumbs at the top (Navbar) to navigate back.

## 3. Immediate Actions (Permanent Controls)
Every file and folder has immediate buttons availableâ€”no menus required!
*   **Share:** Click the **Network icon** to open the "Share" modal. You can simulate copying a public link here.
*   **Star:** Click the **Star icon** to mark an item as important. It will now appear in your **"Starred"** view in the sidebar.
*   **Rename:** Click the **Edit/Pencil icon** to quickly change the name of any item.
*   **Delete:** Click the **Trash icon** to move an item to the Trash.

## 4. Smart Views
*   **Recent:** Check the **"Recent"** tab in the sidebar to see your 20 most recently created or accessed items.
*   **Starred:** Check the **"Starred"** tab to find everything you've marked as important with a gold glow.

## 5. Storage Management (The Trash)
*   **Restore:** Go to the **"Trash"** view. Click the **Rotate icon** to bring an item back to its original folder.
*   **Permanent Delete:** Click the **X icon** on a trashed item to remove it forever.
*   **Empty Trash:** Click the red **"Empty Trash"** button at the top right of the Trash view to clear everything at once!

---
**Note:** CloudVault uses **Account-Specific Syncing**.
- **Guest State:** When unlinked, your data is stored in your local guest profile.
- **Linked State:** When you link a Google account, CloudVault switches to that account's specific cloud storage. Data you create while logged in is permanently tethered to that email.
- **Auto-Unlinking:** Signing out "unlinks" your session, safely storing your cloud data and restoring your local guest view. Log back in at any time to instantly restore your cloud files!
